A Calculator

In the year 2157, Mars and Venus, once allies in the peaceful exploration and colonization of the outer planets, are plunged into an all-out war.

Tensions between the two planets have been rising for decades, fueled by deep cultural differences, territorial disputes over valuable resources, and the unchecked ambition of their ruling governments. Mars, with its superior technology, vast militarized forces, and harsh authoritarian regime, seeks to dominate the solar system, convinced that their way of life is the key to humanity's survival and progress.

Venus, on the other hand, is a thriving world of lush environments and rich natural resources, where a decentralized, democratic society values freedom, independence, and environmental preservation.
